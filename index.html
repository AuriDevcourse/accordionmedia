• What is TechBBQ?

    TechBBQ is Scandinavia's largest startup and technology summit, focused on connecting startups, investors, industry leaders, and media from around the world. The event fosters collaboration, knowledge sharing, and networking among key players in the startup and tech ecosystem.

  • Why should I come to TechBBQ?

    TechBBQ is a unique opportunity to connect with the Nordic startup and tech ecosystem. By attending, you'll gain access to world-class speakers, groundbreaking startups, and top-tier investors and media from around the globe. The event is designed to foster collaboration, inspiration, and networking, making it ideal for those interested in the latest trends, innovations, and opportunities in startups and tech. Whether you're a journalist looking for compelling stories, an investor scouting the next big thing, or a startup founder aiming to make valuable connections, TechBBQ provides an engaging platform to discover, connect, and grow in Copenhagen, one of the most vibrant tech communities in the world.

  • Who can get a Media Pass - and what are the requirements?

    Media passes are reserved for journalists, videographers, photographers, podcasters, bloggers, and other editorial members of the press who want to cover TechBBQ. With limited availability, we prioritize passes for outlets with a strong following and journalists with a solid track record in covering startups, tech, or venture capital. Please note that PR & Communications professionals, as well as non-editorial media staff, are encouraged to purchase a general attendee ticket. To apply, please fill out the media accreditation form on our press page. Approval is typically sent within 7 days. Media passes are limited.

  • What does a Media Pass include?

    A media pass grants full access to all TechBBQ sessions, keynote presentations, and networking events. Media pass holders may also access the press room, where interviews with speakers and event organizers can be arranged.

  • How can I find breaking news during TechBBQ?

    To stay updated on breaking news during TechBBQ, you can check our official event app "Brella", which will feature real-time announcements, schedule updates, and important notifications. Additionally, follow TechBBQ on our social media channels, where we will post live updates, highlights, and any major announcements throughout the event. For exclusive press updates, visit the media lounge on-site or check your email for our regular press bulletins. If you have specific questions or need immediate information, contact our Head of PR on kba@techbbq.org.

  • Is there an online media kit available?

    Yes, our online media kit includes logos, official photos, press releases, and other resources to assist with your coverage. You can download the full media kit here.

  • Who are the speakers coming to TechBBQ?

    This year's lineup includes prominent speakers from global tech companies, innovative startups, and leading investment firms. For a full list of confirmed speakers, visit our Speakers page here.

  • Can I book interviews with TechBBQ speakers, attendees and organizers?

    Yes, interviews with speakers, organizers, and selected attendees can be arranged. Please contact our Head of PR at kba@techbbq.org to schedule interviews or request specific interviewees. Interviews are typically granted based on speaker availability.

  • Can I bring my wireless equipment and camera/audio gear?

    Yes, media representatives are welcome to bring wireless equipment, cameras, and audio gear to TechBBQ. However, to avoid interference with event operations, we ask that all wireless devices operate within standard frequency ranges. Please also be mindful of our filming and photography guidelines: interviews and filming are generally permitted in designated areas, but capturing event sessions or attendees without prior consent may be restricted. If you have specific technical requirements or need additional support, contact our Head of PR at kba@techbbq.org.

  • Are there any guidelines for media coverage at the event?

    We ask that all media representatives follow our guidelines on respectful and accurate coverage of TechBBQ and our participants. Filming and photography is permitted in designated areas, while it should be avoided capturing attendees who explicitly request privacy. For full guidelines, please refer to our Media Policy here.
